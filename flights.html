Earth Orbit

Being able to see the planet in a single view will take your breath away; from the snow capped tips of the poles, deepest of blues of our oceans, the lush green landscapes and yellow scorched sands of the hottest deserts overlaid with the everchanging swirls created by the global jet streams – the colours of which are amplified against the backdrop of deep dark space.
Each flight is guaranteed to provide two orbits of the Earth. The pre-determined trajectory path crosses the globe in a seemingly diagonal pattern ensuring visibility across all continents – passing over Earth’s equator up-to 4 times!
Every flight also includes ‘Astro:Zero’ a unique opportunity to unshackle the harness and experience zero gravity. During this time you’ll be free to choose how you wish to mark the moment, whether that’s participating with the on-board Astro:Science team’s latest research, part-taking in a game of Zero:Ball or simply living out a child hood dream of ‘flying’. The onboard meal served at this time is also sure to become the most memorable (if not messy) meal of your life!
